The Best Movies Of All Time According to Geraden Christensen

My top 3 movies of all time are....

Jurassic Park

An ageless classic that's not just dinosaurs, but begs the question to it's viewers, should man have this kind of power?

Forrest Gump

One man's lifelong journey to finding the love of his life and somehow being involved in every major event in modern history on the way there.

Logan

Forget Infinity War and Endgame, this is the best superhero movie ever made.
